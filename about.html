GIS Specialist, 3D GIS , Web GIS AR & VR Developer

Hello, I'm Recep, I graduated from Sakarya University, Department of Geography in 2017. Throughout my education, I have had the opportunity to encounter many GIS softwares as I took part in many activities and projects within the scope of GIS. In the year I graduated, I was started to work as a 3D Product Specialist at Esri Turkey.Along the period that I have been an employee at Esri Turkey, I have gained both theoretical and practical experience on high scale city modelling, Geographical Information Systems, 3D GIS and advanced 3D models , virtual reality and augmented reality. Now I have intensive knowledge on 3D GIS and ArcGIS CityEngine. I have extensive Esri Turkey experience, superb 3B GIS skills and a sound knowledge of CityEngine. My area of key qualifications are;

  • Conceptualizing the design and create 3D levels for a game.
  • Has excellent 3D Visualization skills. Turn geospatial data into 3D representations with multiple layers of useful findings.
